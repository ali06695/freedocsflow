True PDF-to-DOCX conversion requires a server-side engine. It's not possible to do accurately in a browser. Our PDF-to-Text tool extracts the raw text content, which you can paste into Word.
There's no enforced limit — it depends on your device's RAM. Large PDFs (over 200MB) may be slow on mobile. For most everyday files the tools are near-instant.
Once the page has loaded, most tools work offline since all processing is in-browser. The only online requirement is the initial page load and the font/library downloads.
Browser-based PDF encryption has limitations. The pdf-lib library's encryption support varies by PDF viewer. For legally binding document protection, a dedicated server-side tool is recommended. We show the warning so you're not misled.
